"Welcome to DEV-OPS"


WE TRAIN THE NEXT GENERATION OF SOFTWARE DEVELOPERS STARTING EVERY FALL.

Every year, we provide less privileged senior high school graduates who are not able to enter the university due to financial or academic issues the opportunity to become software developers.

Applications open on 16.02.2023

Main Programs

Mobile App Development with Flutter

Flutter is an open source framework by Google for building beautiful, natively compiled, multi-platform applications from a single codebase.

Web Development

Web developers program websites and ensure they perform reliably and efficiently. we train you to become a full-stack web developer or a front-end developer.

Data Analytics with Excel

In this program, you'll be introduced to the world of data analytics through hands-on curriculum developed by Google. You'll develop in-demand data analytics

UX/UI with graphic design

Get ready for UX Design jobs with hands-on assignments. Learn how to conduct user research. Gain the skills, confidence, and experience needed to launch your career in UX Design.
